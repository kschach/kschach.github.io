Theoretical and Conceptual Framework

  • Socioeconomic justice
    • Fraser (1997)
      • Redistribution and recognition
      • Affirmation and transformation
    • Noonan (2012)
      • “life-value”
      • Engagement with current institutions to maximize life-value
  • Socioeconomic justice through the welfare state
    • vs. antecedent charity model
    • vs. neoliberal retrenchment
  • Socioeconomic justice through nonprofit social service
    • vs. through public provision
      • the costs of detachment from the state
    • vs. through social movement
      • the costs of attachment to the state
